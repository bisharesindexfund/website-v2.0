How Staking Works

Deposit

Stake your BISON by depositing your tokens in the BISON Pool located in the BiShares farm.

Earn

Collect a portion of all fees generated from the BiShares platform based on your stake percentage of the pool.

Redeem

Harvest your BISON, re-stake, or sell your staking award on the open market.

Staking Rewards & Dividends

Stake BISON to earn more BISON via the BISON pool. Receive a share of the allocated BISON emissions in proportion to your stake in the pool.

All platform fees are collected in the BiShares treasury from every mint, burn, and ArbEx trade. A portion of all platform fees are then redistributed to the community via the staking contract. Receive a dividend airdrop of BUSD directly to your wallet once a month in proportion to your staked amount in the BISON Pool.

*All staking mechanics, including the criteria for staking, are subject to future change.
